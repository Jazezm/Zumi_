About the Educational Tour

Our one-day educational tour in Davao was both informative and engaging. We visited three key institutions: Vitro Inc., Jairosoft Inc., and Davao Central 911. At Vitro Inc., we learned about modern data center operations and how secure digital infrastructure supports various industries. Our visit to Jairosoft Inc. gave us insights into software development processes, workplace collaboration, and the role of technology in creating real-world solutions.

Lastly, at Davao Central 911, we were introduced to the city’s emergency response system and how technology and coordination help ensure public safety. Overall, the tour enhanced our understanding of how information technology is applied in professional and government settings.
